STEVE JOBS

1955 - 2011

A visionary entrepreneur, inventor, and the co-founder of Apple Inc., whose innovations transformed the technology industry. Known for his passion, creativity, and relentless pursuit of excellence, Jobs played a pivotal role in shaping the personal computing revolution, the creation of the iPod, iPhone, and iPad, and the reimagining of digital content with iTunes.
