STEVE JOBS

1955 - 2011

A visionary entrepreneur, inventor, and the co-founder of Apple Inc., whose innovations transformed the technology industry. Known for his passion, creativity, and relentless pursuit of excellence, Jobs played a pivotal role in shaping the personal computing revolution, the creation of the iPod, iPhone, and iPad, and the reimagining of digital content with iTunes.

Timeline of Steve Jobs' Key Milestones

1976

Co-founded Apple Inc. with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne, introducing the Apple I, the first personal computer kit.

1977

Launched the Apple II, one of the first highly successful mass-produced microcomputers.

1980

Apple went public, making Jobs a multimillionaire at the age of 25.

1984

Introduced the Macintosh, the first personal computer with a graphical user interface (GUI), which revolutionized the industry.

1985

Left Apple after a power struggle with the board of directors, later founding NeXT Inc., a computer platform development company.

1986

Acquired The Graphics Group, later known as Pixar Animation Studios, which became a leader in computer-generated animation.

1996

Apple acquired NeXT, bringing Jobs back to the company as an advisor, and later as the interim CEO.

1998

Released the iMac, a game-changing all-in-one computer known for its design and ease of use, marking the beginning of Apple's resurgence.

2001

Launched the iPod, revolutionizing the music industry and paving the way for Apple's dominance in portable media devices.

2003

Introduced the iTunes Store, transforming the way people purchase and consume digital media.

2007

Unveiled the first iPhone, a groundbreaking product that redefined smartphones and became one of the best-selling consumer products of all time.

2010

Launched the iPad, creating a new category of mobile devices and further solidifying Apple's position as an industry leader.

2011

Resigned as CEO of Apple due to health issues but continued to serve as Chairman of the Board. Passed away on October 5, 2011, leaving behind a legacy of innovation that continues to inspire.
